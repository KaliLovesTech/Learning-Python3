Python Math Symbols and Their Explanations

+  : Addition
-  : Subtraction
*  : Multiplication
/  : Division
%  : Modulus (remainder of division)
** : Exponentiation (power)
// : Floor Division (division that rounds down to the nearest integer)

<  : Less than
>  : Greater than
<= : Less than or equal to
>= : Greater than or equal to
== : Equal to
!= : Not equal to
